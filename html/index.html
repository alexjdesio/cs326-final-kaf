About Orchard Grove:

Orchard Grove is a wonderful home for pets of all sizes, types and species. The staff are well paid and are therefore more likely to be kind to the pets that stay here. Orchard Grove is a wonderful and well respected place to adopt from. Pets are literally jumping for joy at the mere mention of the name Orchard Grove. This is because of the work of the founder Jim Wallace, whose love for beagles almost drove him mad, until he began to spread his love and joy of pets to the rest of the world.
