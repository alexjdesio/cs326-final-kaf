Sparky
Westminster Animal Shelter

Sparky is a Boston Terrier mixed with an unknown breed of hound. He was abandoned when he was just 6 months old, and was rescued a few weeks later when a friend of the shelter found him sleeping on a street corner. Even after his sad upbringing, Sparky is a joyous, energetic and playful dog. Since his rescue, he has regained a healthy weight and coat of fur, but he still has some extra needs for love and attention.
