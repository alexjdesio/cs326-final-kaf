Sparky

Westminster Animal Shelter (this should link to the page)

Sparky is a Boston Terrier mixed with an unknown breed of hound. He was abandoned when he was just 6 months old, and was rescued a few weeks later when a friend of the shelter found him sleeping on a street corner. He is now one year old. Even after his sad upbringing, Sparky is a joyous, energetic and playful dog.

Since his rescue, he has regained a healthy weight and coat of fur, but he still has some extra needs for love and attention. If you wish to adopt Sparky, make sure you read up on his health concerns--he may be at a proper weight, but he requires some extra care from his future owners.
